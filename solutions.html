Overcoming Stagnation

Many small and medium-sized enterprises experience a loss of momentum and stagnating growth. Stagnant growth and a lack of innovation leads to a loss in market share. This, in turn, leads to a decrease in revenue and profits—even leading to regression or possible business failure.

Some founders feel overwhelmed by the complexity of running a small/medium size organisation without any clear goals or plans for how to grow their business. They get stuck in a mire of administrative tasks with no end in sight. It's hard to stay motivated and get things done when you're not sure why or what you want to get done! The problem of stagnation can be disastrous. This can lead to under-performance, loss of innovation, and missed opportunities.

Organisational ReWilding is a tested methodology that can help organisations solve any problems related to complexity and stagnation.
